Kind

A mobile application created by Michael Cane through the Ionic Framework that provides a time and stress management toolset for young people dealing with anxiety and depression.

By providing a non-shaming Milestone driven achievement system and a continuous, but subtle, approach to the attention to the user's mental state this application should provide a tool for teens to properly deal with the stress they deal with on a day to day basis. Through guided mental and physical excersises this application will provide an unaggressive and non-fear-driven outlet for anxiety and depression.
